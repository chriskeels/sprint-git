BrightPath Tutoring
BrightPath Tutoring is a company that helps middle and high school Associates with math and science. They have an online app where Associates log in, watch videos, take quizzes, and track their progress.
One day, Associates started complaining.
Some Associates say:
“The app won’t load.”
“My quiz disappeared.”
“It worked yesterday but not today.”
The teachers are confused. The app works on one computer, but not on another. When the team tries to fix it, they accidentally break something else. Every time they update the app, something new goes wrong.
Parents get upset. Associates lose trust. The company starts losing money.
The Business Problem
BrightPath has three big problems:
The app works differently on different computers.
Updates sometimes break the system.
They don’t know right away when something fails.
Because of this:
Associates can’t finish homework.
Teachers can’t track grades.
Support tickets pile up.
The company looks unprofessional.
How This Project Solves It
Using Docker, Docker Compose, and CI:
The app runs the same way everywhere.
The website and database start together correctly.
Every time someone updates the code, the system tests itself automatically.
If something breaks, the team sees it right away.
Now:
Updates are safer.
Problems are caught early.
Associates can always log in.
Parents trust the platform.

Why This Matters
For a Associates-facing company, even one hour of downtime can mean:
Missed homework
Frustrated Associatess
Lost customers
